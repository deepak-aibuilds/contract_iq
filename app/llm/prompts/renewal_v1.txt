### Field Definitions

* `is_auto_renewal`: true only if the contract explicitly states automatic renewal, extension, or rollover.
* `notice_required`: notice period required to prevent renewal (e.g., "30 days written notice").
* `renewal_terms`: renewal duration or conditions (e.g., "renews for successive one-year terms", "same terms unless renegotiated").
* `raw_text`: exact renewal clause from the contract.
* `confidence`:

  * `high` = renewal terms clearly stated
  * `medium` = renewal language exists but details are ambiguous
  * `low` = no renewal information found
* `is_found`: true if any renewal-related provision exists, otherwise false.
